Facts About Dogs

Dogs are highly intelligent, social animals domesticated over 20,000 years ago, with roughly 700 million to 1 billion worldwide. They possess a sense of smell 40x to 100,000x more sensitive than humans, can learn over 100 words/gestures, and show genuine love by releasing oxytocin when interacting with their owners.

A study was conducted on dogs being away from their owners for varying hours and the studies show that dogs who were away from their owners the longest showed the greatest amount of affection!
